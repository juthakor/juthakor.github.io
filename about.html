About

Fascinated by the endless possibilities of software development and its power to shape the world around us, I decided to pivot my career and pursue my passion for programming.


With my strong foundation in physics, I bring a unique perspective to the field of technology. I have a knack for solving complex problems and a keen eye for detail, honed through years of working with scientific formulas and equations. I have leveraged this skill set to become a proficient programmer, capable of writing efficient, elegant code that can tackle even the most intricate of challenges.


My experience in physics has also taught me the importance of collaboration and the power of teamwork. I am a dedicated team player who values communication and cooperation in achieving our shared goals. I believe in staying current with the latest trends and techniques in the field, and am always eager to learn new things and expand my skill set.


In short, my passion for technology, coupled with my background in physics, has made me a well-rounded and skilled programmer with a unique perspective and a drive for success.
